Presentations in Cyblog

A demo.

Usage

Apply the template with @template presentation.

Add the meta-presentation-header and meta-presentation-footer declarations to your document's meta block. Click the right half of the screen or use the right- / down- arrow keys to advance, click the left half or use the left- / up- arrow keys to go back. Cyblog inserts pre- and post-presentation slides to your document automatically. You can also swipe between slides.

Adding slides

Create slides by adding blocks with the .presentation-slide class! It's that simple. All cyblog markup is supported.
